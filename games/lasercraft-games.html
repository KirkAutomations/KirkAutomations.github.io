About LaserCraft

LaserCraft has been precision fabricating in Orion Township, Michigan since 1995. They operate 3 CNC laser machines (including two 5-axis units), presses from 20 to 600 tons, robotic welding cells, CNC machining centers, and a full CAD/CMM inspection department. ISO 9001 certified.

From raw steel to finished assembly — stamp, cut, machine, weld, inspect — all under one roof.
