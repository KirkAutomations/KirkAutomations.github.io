FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of companies does Bankers Edge Advisory work with? +

We work with middle-market companies, private equity sponsors, and financial institutions across a range of sectors. Our clients typically seek debt capital solutions, loan portfolio optimization, or strategic M&A advisory services.

What is bridge financing and when is it appropriate? +

Bridge financing provides short-term capital to cover immediate needs while longer-term financing is arranged. It's commonly used in acquisitions, restructurings, and situations where speed of execution is critical to capturing an opportunity.

How is Bankers Edge Advisory different from larger investment banks? +

As a boutique firm, we provide senior-level attention on every engagement. Our managing partners are directly involved in every transaction, bringing institutional-grade expertise with the personalized service and responsiveness that larger firms often cannot match.

What credentials do your team members hold? +

Both managing partners are CFA Charterholders with FINRA Series 63, 79, and 82 licenses. They hold advanced degrees from the University of Michigan and bring over 30 years of combined experience in capital markets and investment banking.

What is a forward flow arrangement? +

A forward flow arrangement is an agreement between an originator and a buyer to purchase loans on a recurring basis at pre-negotiated terms. This provides originators with a reliable capital source and buyers with a consistent pipeline of assets.
