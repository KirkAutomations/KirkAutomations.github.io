Who We Are

The Edge in Your Next Capital Markets Transaction

Bankers Edge Advisory is a boutique investment banking firm specializing in debt capital markets. With over 30 years of combined experience, our managing partners bring deep expertise in fixed income, risk management, and secondary market transactions.

Our team has worked closely with corporate, banking, and insurance clients to solve complex liquidity and risk management challenges. We leverage our extensive network of institutional relationships to deliver bespoke capital solutions.

Whether you're seeking bridge financing, portfolio optimization, or strategic M&A guidance, Bankers Edge Advisory provides the personalized attention and institutional-grade expertise your transaction demands.

Our Team

Meet the Managing Partners

Richard Consul, CFA

Managing Partner

Richard Consul has partnered with many corporate, banking, and insurance clients and helped them solve a variety of liquidity, risk management, and secondary market challenges. He has over two decades' worth of experience and domestic and international secondary market expertise from working as a Senior Portfolio Manager and Fixed-Income, Currency, and Commodities Strategist.

Richard earned a BBA in Finance from the University of Michigan and a MSE in Financial Engineering from the University of Michigan. He is a member of the CFA Institute and currently holds a FINRA Series 63, 79, and 82 License. Richard is a Registered Representative of BA Securities, LLC. Member FINRA SIPC.

raconsul@bankersedgeadvisory.com (248) 953-5163 in LinkedIn

Mitch Vermet, CFA, CAIA

Managing Partner

Mitch Vermet has assisted clients from various sectors by crafting strategic asset liability management solutions that have helped them manage risks and better structure their balance sheets. He has ten years of experience working closely with clients in institutional asset management and investment banking and has been on teams responsible for the allocation of over $30 billion in institutional capital.

Mitch earned a BA in Economics from the University of Michigan and a MA in Management from the Ross School of Business at the University of Michigan. Mitch also earned a MS in Data Science from the School of Engineering at Wayne State University. He is a member of the CFA Institute, the CAIA Association, and currently holds a FINRA Series 63, 79, and 82 License. Mitch is a Registered Representative of BA Securities, LLC. Member FINRA SIPC.
