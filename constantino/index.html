The Plan

Four Fights for NY-21

Not talking points. Not focus-grouped pablum. Real priorities for the district, from someone who’s actually built something.

Border Security

NY-21 shares a border with Canada. Unsecured borders mean fentanyl in our communities and strain on local resources. Anthony will fight for real enforcement, not performative politics.

Broadband Expansion

Half of Upstate New York can’t get reliable internet. In 2026, that’s unacceptable. Anthony knows tech. He built a tech company here. He’ll bring real broadband infrastructure to every corner of the district.

Affordability & Tax Relief

New Yorkers are taxed into the ground. Groceries, gas, property taxes—everything costs more and pays less. Anthony will fight to cut taxes, reduce regulation, and bring real relief to working families.

Fort Drum & Military Support

Fort Drum is the backbone of the North Country. Anthony will fight for military funding, veteran services, and infrastructure enhancements that keep our soldiers supported and our community strong.
