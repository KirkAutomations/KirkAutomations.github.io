🛡️ Objection Handlers

"We're already on SolidWorks"

"SolidWorks is great for where you are now. But as your designs get more complex, you'll hit its ceiling — especially in surfacing and simulation. Solid Edge gives you synchronous technology with a smooth migration path. And NX is there when you need enterprise power."

"Siemens is too expensive"

"Let's look at total cost of ownership. Siemens integrates CAD+CAM+PLM in one ecosystem — no third-party bolt-ons, no data translation errors, no lost time. Our ROI calculator usually shows payback in 8-14 months."

"We're looking at Fusion 360"

"Fusion is attractive on price, but look at what's missing: robust PLM, advanced simulation, and manufacturing-grade CAM for complex multi-axis. You'll outgrow it — and migrating away from Autodesk later costs more than starting right."

"Our team doesn't have time for training"

"That's exactly why we offer Xcelerator Academy — self-paced, on-demand, fits into any schedule. Plus our custom on-site sessions are tailored to YOUR workflows, not generic. We've trained teams at Trico, Pratt & Miller — they see productivity gains within weeks."

"We only need CAD, not all this other stuff"

"Totally fair. Solid Edge is standalone-ready and priced for exactly that. But having the option to grow into CAM, simulation, and PLM without switching platforms — that's the long game. No re-training, no data migration headaches down the road."
